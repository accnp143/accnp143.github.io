Respiratory Calculators

Desired FiO₂ Calculation

New FiO₂ = (Current FiO₂ × Desired PaO₂) / Actual PaO₂

Tidal Volume (VT) & IBW

VT = 6-8 ml/kg (IBW)
IBW = 50(M) or 45.5(F) + 0.91 × (Height in cm - 152.4)

Desired Respiratory Rate (RR)

New RR = (Current RR × Current PaCO₂) / Desired PaCO₂

PaO₂/FiO₂ Ratio (P/F Ratio)

Ratio = PaO₂ / FiO₂
Normal >300 | Mild 200-300 | Moderate 100-200 | Severe <100
